Our Mission

Smart Agri is designed to help farmers monitor and manage their crops efficiently. Our system provides real-time data on farm sections, tree health, and fertilization schedules.

Features

  • Real-time monitoring of farm sections.
  • Detailed tree health analysis.
  • Fertilization and watering schedules.
  • User-friendly dashboard for easy management.
